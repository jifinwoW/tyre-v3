Press On Band Tyres

Solid press on tires, also known as cushion tires, are designed specifically to meet the requirements of tough material handling applications. They are puncture free, highly resistant to wear and over one of the lowest cost of ownership. Press on tires further enhance forklifts stability, load capacity and service life of equipment. They are made of a rubber tread layer bonded to a cylindrical steel band and then mounted onto a steel hub using a press.
